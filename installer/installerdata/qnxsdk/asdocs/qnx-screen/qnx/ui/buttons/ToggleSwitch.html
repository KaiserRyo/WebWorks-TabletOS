Classpublic class ToggleSwitch
InheritanceToggleSwitch Slider UIComponent flash.display.MovieClip

The ToggleSwitch represents a button that has one of two states indicating an "ON" or "OFF" type state.

A toggle switch has a thumb that can be dragged like a Slider but differs in functionality where if you click the track or background of the Toggle Switch, it will change the selected state of the switch.

A toggle switch is comprised of a track, thumb and fill and two text labels.
